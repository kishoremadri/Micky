About Us

MickyMarvels LLC

MickyMarvels LLC was founded with one simple mission: make Agile, Scrum, and Kanban practical and accessible for every professional who wants to grow in their career.

We believe the best mentoring happens in the real world — not just in training rooms or slide decks. Our approach combines proven frameworks with genuine on-the-job experience, giving you tools that actually work in your daily environment.

Whether you're a new Scrum Master, an experienced project coordinator, or a team trying to improve delivery — we meet you exactly where you are and take you where you want to go.

Our Services

Agile Services Tailored to You

From individual coaching to full team transformation — every service is built around real outcomes.

🎯

Agile Coaching

Practical guidance for professionals and teams improving collaboration, delivery speed, and Agile culture. Ideal for teams adopting Agile for the first time or looking to improve consistency.

Who it's for: Teams, Agile leads, PMs transitioning to Agile roles

Key benefits: Better sprint outcomes, clearer roles, improved team dynamics and communication

🔄

Scrum Mentoring

Deep dive into Scrum roles, events, artifacts, and how Scrum truly works inside real organizations. Learn from someone who has lived it — not just studied it.

Who it's for: New Scrum Masters, aspiring Product Owners, dev teams new to Scrum

Key benefits: Confidence in Scrum ceremonies, real-world role clarity, certification preparation

📊

Kanban Guidance

Learn how to visualize work, limit WIP, manage flow, and identify bottlenecks before they slow your team down. Practical, board-level coaching that drives results.

Who it's for: Operations teams, support teams, and mixed-method Agile teams

Key benefits: Improved flow, reduced cycle time, visual team clarity, and less firefighting

💼

On-the-Job Mentoring

Your real job, your real challenges. This is mentoring that happens alongside your actual daily work — not in a classroom. Navigate team dynamics, delivery pressure, and career decisions with a trusted advisor by your side.

Who it's for: Anyone in an Agile environment who needs direct, ongoing real-world support

Key benefits: Immediate application, ongoing accountability, clear career direction and confidence
